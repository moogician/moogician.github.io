**Theorem 1** (*Markov's Inequality*) 
\begin{equation}
    $$X \ge 0 \wedge \mathbb{E}[X] < + \infty \Rightarrow \forall k>0.\prob{}{X \ge k} \le \frac{\mathbb{E}[X]}{k}$$
\end{equation}
