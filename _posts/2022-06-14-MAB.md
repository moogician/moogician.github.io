## Setting
In a stochastic multi-armed bandit (MAB) problem a gambler needs to choose at each round of play one of k arms, each characterized by an unknown reward distribution. Reward realizations are only observed when an arm is selected, and the gambler’s objective is to maximize the cumulative expected earnings after T rounds. To regulate the problem, we assume that we get a loss instead of a reward at each pull. At round $t$, the player choose an arm $a_i$ and observe a loss $l_t(a_i)$.
