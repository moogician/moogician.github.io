I am a first-year Ph.D. student in computer science at University of California, Berkeley, temporarily advised by [Koushik Sen](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~ksen/?rnd=1730334694196). I also worked on quantum computing with [Tongyang Li](https://www.tongyangli.com/) with a focus on optimization algorithms.
